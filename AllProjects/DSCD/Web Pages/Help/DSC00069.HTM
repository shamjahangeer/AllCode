Determine JIT Quantities

To determine the number of JIT shipped schedules in daily shipment totals

  1. If a shipped schedule has JIT shipments within its total (denoted by a J above the total), position your cursor on that daily total of schedules shipped.

    Note: A J positioned above a daily total indicates there are just-in-time (JIT) shipments within that total. JIT schedules are measured to a window of 0 Days Early and 0 Days Late, regardless of Standard Default or Customer Variable window. The Early, On-Time, and Late-shipments and percentages will show JIT as early, on-time, or late to a 0 Days Early/Days Late window.

  2. Position the cursor on the total of schedules shipped (the quantity not the character J). A pop-up window displays showing the number of JIT shipments and Non-JIT shipments that comprise that shipment total.
