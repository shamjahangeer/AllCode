Data View Categories

Within each major data view, you may have one or more view categories. These categories vary depending on the major data view you have chosen.

Category names reflect Delivery Scorecard window types and will include one or more of the following:

  • Customer (account number) List

  • Lead Time (schedules)
  • List (list summary)
  • Nonconforming (schedules)
  • Conforming (schedules)
  • Open (schedules)
  • Open Summary
  • Open Nonconforming

     
     

  • Organization Year-to-Date List

  • Past due (schedules)
  • Schedule Detail
  • Summary (graphical summary)
  • Worldwide (account number) List

     

To select a data view category

Click the desired category.
