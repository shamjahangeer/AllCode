If a Shipment Was on Credit Review

If a shipment was on Credit Review, performance is tracked from the Credit Off Date or from the Schedule Date, whichever is the latest. One 'grace day' is added to the Commit Date for calculation purposes only if a shipment is already late.

If the Credit Off Date is greater than or equal to the Schedule Date and the shipment is already late, the calculation is Credit Off Date plus 1 day, unless the Shipped Date = the Credit Off Date.

If the Credit Off Date is before the Schedule Date, the calculation is Commit Date to Shipped Date.
